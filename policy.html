Official Statement

Purpose of the Application

Our application is made for the comfortable use of the Quran and accurate prayer times. It is designed to help users read, learn, and practice with confidence, whether they are at home, traveling, or visiting a mosque.

We are an independent educational and spiritual service. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any political movement, armed group, or extremist organization. We do not represent such groups and we stand firmly against terrorism, violence, and hatred.

The app exists for peaceful worship and learning only. Any content or activity that promotes harm, intimidation, or the glorification of violence is prohibited. If misuse is detected or reported, we reserve the right to restrict access and to cooperate with lawful requests where required.

Our goal is to keep a respectful environment focused on faith and education. If you notice content or behavior that conflicts with this policy, please reach out so we can review it and respond promptly.

  • Purpose: easy and reliable access to the Quran and prayer times.
  • Independence: no political or extremist affiliation.
  • Safety: zero tolerance for violence, hate, or intimidation.
  • Reporting: contact us if you see misuse or harmful content.
Non-Affiliation

Ummah Online does not support any extremist ideology. We oppose the misuse of religious teachings to justify harm or hatred.

Respect and Peace

We promote respectful learning and personal spiritual growth. The platform is built for calm reflection and positive practice.

Accountability

We monitor reports, act when needed, and cooperate with lawful requests to keep the service safe for all users.

Contact and Reporting

If you have concerns or would like to report misuse, please contact our team. We review every report and respond as quickly as possible.
